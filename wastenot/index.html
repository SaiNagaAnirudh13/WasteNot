About Us

Transforming Surplus into Sustenance

Welcome to WasteNot, a revolutionary platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting donors with recipients in need. Our mission is to create a community where surplus food can be redistributed efficiently, ensuring that no edible food goes to waste and more people have access to the nourishment they need.
